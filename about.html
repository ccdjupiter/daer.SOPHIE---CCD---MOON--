However, I wanted to take my coding skills to the next level and decided to enroll in the SheCodes program. With its supportive learning environment for women, I was able to refine my skills and knowledge in web development and build a strong foundation to tackle new challenges.

In addition to my creativity, I am a highly motivated and detail-oriented individual who thrives in a collaborative work environment. Whether working on a team or independently, I am always eager to take on new challenges and contribute my skills and expertise to create outstanding projects.
