WEATHER APP

My weather app tracks the user location whilst displaying weather data of the world. The created weather-app Utilizes openweathermap.org API. By default the weather-app displays the local weather at the geo-point location of current individuals placements. Individualsbcan use the search form to find current weather at a specific city/country.


The app displays forecast by days, hours whilst showing the last updated times, temperatures, and brief descriptions: such as humidity, wind direction, and weather icons for each days based on the weather forecast. Finally, temperatures is represented in Celsius and Fahrenheit units. My weather-app is built utilizing html, javascript, APIs, vanilla JS and OpenWeather API.
